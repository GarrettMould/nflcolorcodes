New Orleans Saints
New Orleans is nothing if not a unique city, and their NFL teams dons the unique combination of black and gold. The Saints won the Super Bowl in 2010, led by future hall-of-fame quarterback Drew Brees. The Brees era, however, came to an end after the 2020 NFL season, and the Saints are now led by the Heisman winner Jameis Winston.
