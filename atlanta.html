Atlanta Falcons
When the Atlanta Falcons entered the league in 1965, they looked to make an immediate connection with the state of Georgia, and they did it with their colors. Their red and black color scheme was similar to the most famous football team in the state, the Georgia Bulldogs. While the Falcons have made slight changes to their colors over the years, their main colors remain the same: red, black, and silver.
