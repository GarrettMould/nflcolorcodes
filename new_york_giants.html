New York Giants
The New York Giants are known as "Big Blue", but the team didn't always wear blue uniforms. In the early days of the franchise, the New York Giants wore red as their primary color, but that changed in 1953, when they adopted blue as their main color. The Giants enjoyed success in the early 2000's, with two Super Bowl wins under the leadership of quarterback Eli Manning.
