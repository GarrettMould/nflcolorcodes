Jacksonville Jaguars
The Jacksonville Jaguars are one of the youngest NFL franchises, their inaugural season coming in 1995. The team wears a unique color scheme of teal and gold, and recently unveiled a new set of uniforms that gives them a modern look. The team is currently in a rebuilding phase, with rookie quarterback Trevor Lawrence and first-year head coach Urban Meyer.
