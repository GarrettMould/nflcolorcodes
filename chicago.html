Chicago Bears
After moving to Chicago in the early 1920's, the Bears changed their colors to orange and blue in honor of George Halas' alma mater, the University of Illinois. The cold Chicago climate and defensive stars like Brian Urlacher and Khalil Mack have given the Bears a tough-nosed reputation, despite their lack of recent success.
