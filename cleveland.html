Cleveland Browns
The solid orange helmets might seem odd on a team called the Browns, but the Cleveland football team is named after their former coach, Paul Brown, rather than the color. For quite some time, the Cleveland Browns have been known for two things: their simple, orange, brown and white color scheme, and putting together dismal records year after year. The latter seems to be a thing of the past though, as the Browns are considered Super Bowl contenders entering the 2021 NFL season.
