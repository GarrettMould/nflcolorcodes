New England Patriots
The New England Patriots rose to relevancy in 2001, when Tom Brady took over for an injured Drew Bledsoe. In the years since, the Patriots have been one of the most successful sports franchises in the world, winning six super bowls (tied for the most in NFL history).They did it all while wearing their signature red, blue, and silver uniforms.
