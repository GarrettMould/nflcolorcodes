Indianapolis Colts
The Colts organization was founded in Baltimore in 1953 and moved to Indianapolis in the 1980s. The team sports a simple uniform design - solid white jerseys with blue sleeve stripes. Their helmet, perhaps the most recognizable part of the uniform, features the team's horseshoe logo.
