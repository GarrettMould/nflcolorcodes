Minnesota Vikings
The Minnesota Vikings are one of two teams in the NFL to wear the color purple, the other being the Baltimore Ravens. Their helmets feature the iconic Viking horns, which have been worn by NFL greats including Randy Moss and Brett Farve. The Vikings have failed to make the playoffs over the past two seasons, but look to make improvements this year, lead by quarterback Kirk Cousins and star running back Dalvin Cook.
