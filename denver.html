Denver Broncos
While John Elway, Terrell Davis, and a string of 1990's super bowl wins have made the Broncos orange and blue uniforms famous, this wasn't always the team's color scheme. When the team was first founded, they sported brown and yellow jerseys. After a dismal first few years though, the team adopted their current colors and have enjoyed consistent success in the league.
