Miami Dolphins
The modern Miami Dolphins are not quite as successful as the Dan Marino led teams of the 1980's, but they do play in style. Their uniforms are a unique combination of aqua and orange, with a recent update giving a modern touch to the classic design. The Dolphins have been on the brink of success in recent years and are looking for their young quarterback Tua Tagovailoa to lead them to the playoffs in 2021.
