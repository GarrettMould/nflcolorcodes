Kansas City Chiefs
The Kansas City Chiefs sport one of the most recognizable logos in the game, the classic arrowhead. Their jerseys are red and gold, with alternative all-white uniforms which are occasionally worn during road matches. At home, however, the team plays in red jerseys, in front of a home crowd which is known as one of the loudest in the league.
