Cincinnati Bengals
The Cincinatti Bengals have one of the most unique jersey designs in the NFL, with their signature orange and black striped helmets. The color scheme has remained consistent since the teams founding in the 1960's, but the Bengals are still in search of their first Super Bowl win - one of twelve NFL teams yet to win one.
