Carolina Panthers
The Carolina Panthers are a relatively modern NFL franchise, and they boast a fresh color scheme of Carolina Blue, black, and silver. When the Panthers faced off against the Denver Broncos in the 2015 Super Bowl, jersey colors became the center of controvery. Despite being the home team, the Bronocs elected to wear all white, due to their poor record while wearing orange. As a result, the Panthers had to wear black uniforms, despite being winless in the playoffs while weaing black.
