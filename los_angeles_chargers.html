Los Angeles Chargers
The Los Angeles Chargers relocated from San Diego to L.A. after the 2016 NFL season, but their logo and color scheme remained unchanged. Their helmets feature the signature Chargers lighting bolt, and a recent change in uniform style means that the Chargers will now be wearing powder blue jerseys.
